Site Name: Chamber of Commerce Website

The site name "Chamber of Commerce Website" was chosen because it clearly identifies the purpose and audience of the site, serving local businesses and community members in promoting economic development and community engagement.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a comprehensive directory of local businesses, information about upcoming community events, and contact details for the chamber of commerce. It aims to foster business connections, promote local economy, and keep residents informed about community activities.

Scenarios

1. As a local business owner looking to expand my network, how can I find and connect with other businesses in the area through this website?

2. As a community resident interested in local events, how can I discover upcoming activities and get involved in chamber-sponsored initiatives?

Color Schema

Primary color: Blue (#0000FF) - used for headings, text color, and borders to create a professional and trustworthy appearance.

Secondary color: White (#FFFFFF) - used for background to ensure readability and a clean, modern look.

Typography

Font: Arial - selected for its readability and professional appearance. It is used for all body text and headings throughout the document and will be applied consistently across the website for optimal user experience.
